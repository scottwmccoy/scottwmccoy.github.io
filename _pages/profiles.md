- Ingrid Sutter — Geological Engineering (2020–2022). Thesis: *Mapping post-wildfire erosion across California using very high-resolution multitemporal satellite imagery.* → Geotechnical Engineer, Haley &amp; Aldrich (Oahu).
- Nate Delgado — Geology (2019–2022). Thesis: *Quantification of wildfire-induced perturbations to soil-water retention curves and their impact on rainfall runoff.* → Geomorphologist, Cardno.
- Alison Cramer — Geological Engineering (2018–2021). Thesis: *Mapping temporal changes of surface elevation and potentially acid-generating material on abandoned mine lands using remotely piloted aerial systems.* → Environmental Specialist, Resource Concepts Inc.

- Jonathan Jacquet — Hydrological Sciences (2014–2016); Best GPHS M.S., 2016. Thesis: *Quantifying environmental controls on the magnitude of glacial lake outburst floods and the resulting impacts to hydrology and geomorphology: Lago Cachet Dos, Patagonia, Chile.* → Geological Engineer, Balance Hydrologic.
- Brian Anderson — Hydrological Sciences (2014–2016), co-advised. Thesis: *Analysis of fault rupture potential resulting from large-scale groundwater withdrawal: application to Spring Valley, Nevada.* → Environmental consultant.
