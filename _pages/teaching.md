I teach across geological engineering, geomorphology, geomechanics, and quantitative methods. Many of my classes have laboratory components for hands-on engagement with difficult concepts:
computer labs in geomechanics, and field labs in geomorphology and field methods, where we take
students into the landscape with the tools and techniques of my research group.
