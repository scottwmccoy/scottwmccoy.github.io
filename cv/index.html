Teaching

I teach across geological engineering, geomorphology, geomechanics, and quantitative methods in the Department of Geological Sciences & Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno. Courses I have developed and taught include:

  • GE 441/641 — Advanced Geomorphology
  • GE 481/681 — Introduction to Geomechanics
  • GE 106 — Introduction to Geological Engineering
  • GEOL 787 — Numerical Modeling of Processes in Earth Science
  • GEOL 701 — Climate and Tectonics of the Sierra Nevada (graduate special topics)
  • GEOL 260 — Introductory Field Geology

I also lead the department’s graduate seminar (GEOL 790) and the incoming-graduate-student course (GEOL 710).
